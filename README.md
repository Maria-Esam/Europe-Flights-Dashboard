# 🛫 European Flights Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing European flight data from 2016 to 2022, covering 332 airports across 42 countries.

---

## 📊 Dataset
- **Source:** European Flights Dataset
- **Period:** 2016 – 2022
- **Records:** 688,099 rows
- **Fields:** Airports, Countries, Departures, Arrivals, Total Flights, IFR Flights, Season, Day of Week

---

## 📈 Dashboard Contents

### Page 1 – Overview
- KPI Cards: Total Flights, Departures, Arrivals, Total Airports, COVID-19 Impact
- Top 10 Airports by Total Flights
- Monthly Flights Trend 2016–2022
- Flights by Season (Donut Chart)
- Top 10 Airports by Season
- IFR vs Total Flights by Year
- Flights by Country (Map)

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- **Amsterdam, Paris CDG and Frankfurt** are the busiest airports in Europe
- **COVID-19 impact in 2020** caused a -56% drop in total flights vs 2019
- **Summer** is the busiest season with 28% of total flights
- **Friday** is the busiest day of the week
- Recovery started in 2021 and continued through 2022
